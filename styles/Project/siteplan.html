Website Plan

Site Name

Break Dance Hub

This name represents an online community focused on breakdancing, showcasing videos, promoting competitions, and advertising brands.

Optional domain availability: breakdancehub.com

Site Purpose

The site provides a hub for breakdancers, offering competition listings, video showcases, and brand promotions.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find upcoming breakdance competitions near me?
  • How can I submit my breakdance videos to be featured on the site?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #1E1E1E (Used for backgrounds and headers)

Accent Color: #FF4500 (Used for buttons and highlights)

Typography

Heading Font: Montserrat (Used for all headers)

Body Font: Open Sans (Used for paragraphs and general text)
